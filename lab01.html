Janelle Fanpage

This is a fan page dedicated to Janelle Pierzina.

She has appeared on seasons 6, 7, 14, and 22 of the popular reality show, Big Brother.

Janelle was a competition beast in her early seasons!

In Season 6, she won HOH on:

  • Week 7
  • Week 10
  • Week 11 - Part 2

In Season 7, she won HOH on:

  • Week 1
  • Week 4
  • Week 6
  • Week 8

She has won the Power of Veto a total of 7 times!

Unfortunately, she did not win anything on Season 14 or 22.
