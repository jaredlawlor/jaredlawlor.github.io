~Cool Big Brother Data!!!~

Here is a graphic that shows a ranking of the total number of days various houseguests have spent inside the Big Brother house, including international versions

Because Big Brother players are cut off from the outside world, they do not have access to social media, news, or even their families. I find this chart interesting, because it shows which houseguests from across the world have spent the most time within their respective Big Brother house. As you can see, Janelle has spent a combined 200 days inside the US house. Her record has recently been overtaken by Nicole Franzel. Janelle's time on BB is divided into the four separate years when she played: 2005, 2006, 2012, and 2020. She is quite the legend.
